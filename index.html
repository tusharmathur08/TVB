What is
Azure?

For many new comers in the business of app development, cloud provided services helps in a big way to their future aspirations. From providing better version control and services like IAAS,PAAS,SAAS cloud providers are in huge demand these days. Why build something that has already been built is what now everyone is talking about.
It helps you reduce cost by not deploying resources which can be bought on rent basis as per your uses. They will take care of the resources where companies can focus on improving their products and services.
Microsoft is one of the top cloud providing companies, Azure is their cloud computing service listing in top 10 cloud providers in the world with 95% of the Fortune 500 companies trusting their business on it. Fast deployment rates of resources with maximum security and reliability makes Azure grabs attention of top IT firms. Scalability is one of the biggest feature of Azure making resources scale as per consumers need. Even for a school or college student working on project, Azure provides Free 1 month subscription to them where they can utilise their resources and understand the concepts well and thorough. For more click on Azure Portal.
